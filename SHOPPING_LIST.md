## Workshop Flow (no soldering!)

1. **Pick a PCB** - heart, star, or flower
2. **Pick LED colors** - grab from color-sorted trays
3. **Insert LEDs** - push legs through holes (+ marking = long leg)
4. **Bend legs** - use pliers to bend flat on back
5. **Trim legs** - snip excess with wire cutters
6. **Insert battery** - CR2032, flat side up
7. **Test!** - flip switch, LEDs light up
8. **Decorate** - nail polish, stickers, washi tape
9. **Add hardware** - jump ring through hole, attach to keychain/necklace

**Time per person:** ~10-15 minutes
